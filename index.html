Activation Legend

  • Yellow: Sigmoid σ(z) = 1 / (1 + e−z) compresses any real input into (0, 1). Large positive z push σ(z) toward 1; large negatives toward 0. Useful for modeling probabilities.
  • Red: ReLU ReLU(z) = max(0, z) outputs zero for negatives and keeps positives unchanged. Adds non-linearity while preserving gradients for positive activations.
